diplomacy requirements

unilateral declarations:
turn 0: msg is written
turn 0.5: msg is sent
turn 1: msg is received

declarations requiring response:
turn 0: msg1 is written
turn 0.5: msg1 is sent
turn 1: msg1 is received, receiver writes msg2 back
turn 1.5: msg2 is sent
turn 2: msg2 is received by original sender
